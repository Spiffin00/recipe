Ingredients

  • Betty Crocker Cake Mix

  • 1 Cup of Water

  • 1/2 Cup of Vegetable Oil

  • 3 Eggs

  • Chocolate Frosting

  • Vanilla Frosting

  • Food Coloring

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F for shiny metal or glass pan or 325 degrees F for dark or nonstick pan. Grease bottom and sides of pan.

  2. Mix Cake Mix, water, oil, and eggs in a large bowl and beat vigorously by hand for two minutes. Pour into pan.

  3. Bake for 28 - 33 minutes (13"x 9" pan). Cool completely before frosting

  4. Cover the top of the cake in a layer of chocolate icing. Mix white icing with blue and green food coloring to form turquoise.

  5. Draw outline of Sans using chocolate, vanilla, and turquoise icing.
